Who We Are

Thành Nam FC started as a single rented pitch and grew into a full football hub — five fields and a gear shop, used by a community of regular players who show up week after week. Whether you're booking a casual five-a-side or gearing up for league season, we keep the essentials covered: a good surface underfoot, the right equipment, and a place to belong.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make football accessible — affordable field time, honest gear prices, and a welcoming space for players of every level, from first-timers to weekend league regulars.

Our Services

  • Field Rentals: five full-size pitches, bookable by the hour
  • Gear Shop: boots, balls, training equipment, accessories
  • League Hosting: organized weekend leagues for regulars
