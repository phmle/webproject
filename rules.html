Pitch Rules

A few ground rules to keep the game fair and the pitches in good shape.

1. Studded Boots Only

Molded or turf studs required on natural grass pitches. No street shoes.

2. Respect Booking Times

Vacate the pitch promptly at the end of your slot so the next group can start on time.

3. No Glass on the Pitch

Glass bottles and containers are not allowed on or near the playing surface.

4. Shin Guards Recommended

Not mandatory, but strongly recommended for all matches.

5. Group Size Limits

Stick to the recommended group size for your booked pitch to avoid overcrowding.

6. Floodlight Curfew

Night sessions end strictly at 11:00 PM. Lights switch off automatically.

Booking & Payment

Payment

Cash, bank transfer, or e-wallet (Momo/ZaloPay) accepted on arrival, or pay in advance via the contact form.

Cancellations

Free cancellation 4+ hours ahead. Late cancellations forfeit 50% of the booking fee.

Weather Policy

Sessions called off for unsafe weather can be rescheduled free of charge.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to bring my own ball?

A: No — match balls are available to rent at the shop, or you're welcome to bring your own.

Q: Can I rent boots if I don't own a pair?

A: Yes, boot rentals are available at the shop counter, subject to availability.

Q: How far in advance should I book?

A: Evenings and weekends fill up fast — booking 2–3 days ahead is recommended.
